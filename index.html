Warriors to Dubs

Best Season Record

In the 2015-16 season, the Warriors have the record for most wins in a season with a win-loss record of 73-9.

Best Playoff Record

The Warriors also accomplished having the most wins in a playoff tournament run. With 16 wins and 1 loss, the 2016-17 Warriors won the NBA finals.

Stephen Curry

Steph Curry's 3 point shot changed the game of basketball. With breaking multiple 3 point records, including the most 3 pointers made in NBA history, he also is the all-time leading scoring for the Golden State Warriors.

Klay Thompson

Klay Thompson also has a few records under his belt. He has the most 3 pointers made in a single game with 14 3 pointers. He also has the most points made in a single quarter, scoring 37 points and perhaps setting one of the most unbreakable records in the NBA.

Draymond Green

Being the core of the Warrior's defense, Draymond Green won the "Defensive Player of the Year" award in the 2016-17 season.

Steve Kerr

Steve Kerr coached the warriors to help achieve the best season record and most playoff wins. He won the "Coach of the Year" award in the 2015-16 NBA season.
